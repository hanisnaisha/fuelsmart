HOW TO ADD YOUR LOGO IMAGE
==========================

1. Place your logo image in: assets/images/logo.png
   (Support formats: PNG, JPG, SVG)

2. Recommended logo size: 160x160 to 256x256 pixels

3. The app will automatically display your logo in the header

4. If the image is not found, it will show a default truck icon

5. To refresh the app after adding the image:
   - Press 'r' in the Flutter terminal for hot reload

Supported file formats:
- .png (recommended)
- .jpg or .jpeg
- .svg

Note: Make sure your logo has a transparent background (PNG) for best appearance!
